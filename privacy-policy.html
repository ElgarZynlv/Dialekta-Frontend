🏛️ PhilosopherChat — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

PhilosopherChat ("the App") is developed by Elgar Zeynalov. This policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

2. Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or contact information.
  • We do not create user accounts.
  • We do not store your chat conversations on any server.
  • We do not use analytics or third-party tracking tools.

3. Local Storage

The App saves the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your preferred language (e.g., Azerbaijani, Russian)
  • Your preferred theme (dark or light mode)
  • Whether you have completed the onboarding screen

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

4. Chat Messages

Chat messages are sent to a backend server solely to generate a response. Messages are not logged, stored, or used for any other purpose after the response is delivered.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
